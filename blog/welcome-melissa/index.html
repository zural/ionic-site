Welcome Melissa Behrens to the Ionic Team!

Tweet
By @benjsperry on May 06, 2014

Today, I’m thrilled to welcome Melissa Behrens (@meltr0n on Twitter) as Ionic’s Operations Manager. She’s helping our team ‘level up’ by making sure all the working parts of our business are running smoothly and efficiently. In other words, she’s enabling each person to focus on what they do best!

Melissa’s previous life includes a Masters degree in counseling. She’s worked as an Addiction Therapist, and a Research Coordinator at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. However, she has since left that world to form an up-and-coming fashion incubator, the Madison Sewing Studio.

She’s also been known to organize some of Madison’s coolest conferences and fashion runway shows, designs her own jewelry, and hangs out with her pup, Henry: Ionic’s Chief Canine Officer.

Please help me and the rest of the team welcome Melissa to the team!
